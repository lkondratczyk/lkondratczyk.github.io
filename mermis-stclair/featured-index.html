LISTING - Stallaggio Ranch Estate - $4,500,000
A magnificent, dramatic gated estate with over 11,000 square feet located in the Montecito Union School District on over 4 acres in the heart of Montecito. Two stories include 5 bedrooms, 9 baths, (master and most bedrooms first floor) a gourmet kitchen, atrium living room with surrounding balcony above creating an incredible setting for entertaining, 8 fireplaces, and a penthouse tower with a 360 degree view of the mountains and valley. (Two of the bedrooms are in the guest apartment with full kitchen, fireplace and separate entrance.) Add to this a 44 foot swimming pool, attached three-car garage off the circular motor court, and a tack room and corrals for horses. Life just doesn't get better than this.?

LISTING - El Andaluz - $2,175,000
Designed by Jeff Shelton, this El Andaluz home of 2200 square feet features mountain views, high open floor plans with high ceilings, and an open-air courtyard with exotic tile work, balconies, and water features. This elite, Moroccan-style condominium is steps away from theatres, restaurants, and shops. The master bedroom contains a large walk-in closet adjoining a large family/media room with fireplace (possibly another bedroom), one and one half baths, living room, large dining area, balcony patio, and gourmet kitchen with ample storage. The high ceilings, arches, and intricate tilework both inside and outside provide the feeling of living in an elite Moroccan village while enjoying all modern amenities. Add to all this your own two-car garage with a private elevator to your condo, and you can be assured that this "must see" downtown condominium is truly exceptional.
